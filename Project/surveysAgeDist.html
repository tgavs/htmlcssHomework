Surveys Distribution by Age

This plot shows the general age distribution of the surveys.
More than 23% of the surveys were made to people with 65 years old or more. The distribution among the other age bins is very similar with the exception of the 18-25 group that has less than 10% of the total surveys.
We can say that the survey is mor represetative for adult and elder people that also are the ones who are heavy users of the IMSS services.
