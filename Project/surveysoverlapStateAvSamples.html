Satisfaction Simulations Distribution by State

This plot shows the satisfaction distribution for each state of the Mexican Territory. Around 1000 simulations were performed taking random samples of 500 surveys for each state. Almost every distribution is Overlaped which means that we can not reject the hipotesis that the satisfaction across the Mexican Territory has no diference by state. Further analytical tests are required. .
