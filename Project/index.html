This Plot represents a random sample simulation taken from the IMSS 2017 and 2018 Satisfaction Surveys.
Around one hundred satisfaction surveys were perfomed in 2017 and the same amount for 2018.
For each year 1,500 random samples of one thousand surveys were taken to calculate the average satisfaction.
This means that for each year, we calculated the average satisfaction 1,500 times; each time with 1,000 surveys taken at random.

As we can see the axis for 2017 and 2018 are just overlaped in a very small amount of points which it means that for almost every simulation the average for 2017 was diferent from the 2018.
And moreover,if we look very close the average distribution for each year, almost each time the satisfaction index from the 2018 was higher than the one for the year 2017.

We will perform statistical test to proof that in fact the mean for each year is statistically different and conclude that in fact the satisfaction has grown from 2017 to 2017.
